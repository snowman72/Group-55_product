BRING LIGHT TO YOUR LIFE

An electric wheelchair assisted by AI,
made from natural material.

"An IT solution for a better world"
Our team began with an aim to create an IT product that would benefit the society. However, afterwards
we shifted our focus to disabled people, as we were deeply moved by the story about their life.
A large number of handicapped individuals in developing countries, such as Vietnam, are going through
a lot of difficulty in travelling around due to the not fully developed road infrastructure.
